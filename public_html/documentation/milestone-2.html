PWP Milestone 2

Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Welcome Header;

A one to two line introduction that clearly states my name and role as a Front end Developer who specializes in front end design. This welcome message will positioned to the side of a professional headshot of myself.

About Me sub header

This section will detail a little more about myself including where I am from, what I do, along with the skills that I can use to create engaging and accessible websites.

Portfolio section

I will include a carasoul style gallery of links to my other projects and sample material. I will include 4 - 6 links that the user can flip through. The user will be able to click the links and be taken to another page that inlcude my role and contribitions to each project along with text or icons that denote what technologies I used.

Contact

I plan to indluce a simple contact form that will include my professional email address along with additional links to professional social media profiles such as: Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. I will also include a contact form that will allow a user to send me an email to get in touch.

Wireframes

I plan for this site to be responsive on small and large screens, along with the site maintaining one layout that will dynamically adjust to small and large screens.
